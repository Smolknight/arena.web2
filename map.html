Parking Information

The stadium features 1250 parking spaces on its property, including parking for guests that have disabilities. Parking Lots open 4 hours prior to any given event. Our parking areas accept cash or card. Prices for parking tend to vary by event, please check for event specific information or call (623)670-8095 for additional help and information.

Door Times

Unless otherwise stated, all doors open one hour prior to the designated event time to begin seating and checking belongings at the gate. For quickest entry bring small handbags and purses, any larger items will be subjected to additional screening.
