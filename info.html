Please Follow Our School Arena Policies Outlined Here!

  • Prohibited: We prohibit glass containers, weapons, alcoholic beverages, smoking, throwing things from the bleachers, and fighting.

  • Age Policies: Some events require the guests to be over a certain age to have a valid ticket. Check event information before purchase.

  • Searches: Our arena searches guests and their belongings upon entry and may confiscate prohibited items.

  • Food and Drinks: We allow packaged snacks and factory-sealed plastic bottles of non-alcoholic beverages.

  • Service animals: We allow service animals for guests with disabilities.

  • Bags: We allow small hand bags and purses. Larger purses and bags will be subjected to additonal screening.

  • Re-entry: We do not allow re-entry for any events.

Amenities and Provisions

Bathrooms are available in the arena at designated areas.
Trash cans are located inside and outside of stalls.
Food is not provided unless explicitly stated in event details.
